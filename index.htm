Physics Formulas and Units

Centripetal Acceleration

Formula Description Unit
\( a_c = \frac{v^2}{r} \) Centripetal acceleration m/s²
\( v = \sqrt{a_c \cdot r} \) Solve for velocity m/s
\( r = \frac{v^2}{a_c} \) Solve for radius meters (m)

Centripetal Force

Formula Description Unit
\( F_c = \frac{m \cdot v^2}{r} \) Centripetal force Newton (N)
\( m = \frac{F_c \cdot r}{v^2} \) Solve for mass kg
\( v = \sqrt{\frac{F_c \cdot r}{m}} \) Solve for velocity m/s
\( r = \frac{m \cdot v^2}{F_c} \) Solve for radius meters (m)

Gravitational Force

Formula Description Unit
\( F = \frac{G \cdot m_1 \cdot m_2}{r^2} \) Gravitational force Newton (N)
\( m_1 = \frac{F \cdot r^2}{G \cdot m_2} \) Solve for mass 1 kg
\( m_2 = \frac{F \cdot r^2}{G \cdot m_1} \) Solve for mass 2 kg
\( r = \sqrt{\frac{G \cdot m_1 \cdot m_2}{F}} \) Solve for distance meters (m)
\( G = \frac{F \cdot r^2}{m_1 \cdot m_2} \) Solve for gravitational constant Nm²/kg²

Quantity of Heat

Formula Description Unit
\( Q_{th} = m \cdot C \cdot \Delta t \) Quantity of heat Joules (J)
\( m = \frac{Q_{th}}{C \cdot \Delta t} \) Solve for mass kg
\( C = \frac{Q_{th}}{m \cdot \Delta t} \) Solve for specific heat capacity J/kg·°C
\( \Delta t = \frac{Q_{th}}{m \cdot C} \) Solve for temperature change °C

Viscous Flow (Viscosity)

Formula Description Unit
\( F = \eta \cdot A \cdot \frac{v}{d} \) Force in viscous flow Newton (N)
\( v = \frac{F \cdot d}{\eta \cdot A} \) Solve for velocity m/s
\( \eta = \frac{F \cdot d}{A \cdot v} \) Solve for viscosity coefficient Pa·s or N·s/m²
\( d = \frac{F \cdot v}{\eta \cdot A} \) Solve for distance meters (m)
\( A = \frac{F \cdot d}{\eta \cdot v} \) Solve for area m²
